# Account & settings

Open **Settings** by tapping your profile picture at the top of Home. From here you manage your profile, the app's appearance, privacy, and your account. To manage account settings on the web, see [Account settings](/core-concepts/account/settings).

## Profile

- **Edit profile.** Update your display name and full name, and set a profile image and cover image. Take a photo or choose one from your gallery, and remove an image when you want a clean slate.
- **Time zone.** Set your time zone, or turn on **Set timezone automatically** to follow your device.

## Appearance

- **Theme.** Choose **Light**, **Dark**, or **System** to match your device.

## Privacy and storage

- **Privacy screen.** Turn this on to blur the app preview when you switch apps, so your content stays private in the app switcher.
- **Manage Cache.** See how much space the app's cache uses and clear it to free up storage. Clearing the cache does not delete your data in Plane.

## Manage your account

Open **Manage Account** to review your account or delete it.

Deleting your account is permanent. It removes your access to Plane and your account data across every workspace and organization you belong to, with no option for recovery.

To delete your account:

1. Tell us why you are deleting your account.
2. Type **delete my account** to confirm.
3. Continue to schedule the deletion.

::: warning
You cannot delete your account while you are the billing owner or an admin of a workspace. Hand over those roles first. After you confirm, there is a short window during which you can sign back in to cancel the deletion.
:::

## Support, legal, and community

Settings also links you to the **Documentation**, **Terms and Conditions**, **Privacy Policy**, and the Plane **Discord** community.

## Keeping the app up to date

The app checks for updates and prompts you when a new version is available. Some updates are optional and some are required before you can continue. Keep the app updated to get the latest features and fixes.

## Sign out

Tap **Sign Out** in Settings to log out of the app. You can switch between workspaces without signing out by tapping your workspace name on Home.
