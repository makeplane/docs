# Plane AI <Badge type="info" text="Pro" />

Plane AI is your in-app assistant. Ask questions about your work, get help drafting and summarizing, and let it take actions on your behalf when Build mode is on. Open it from the **Plane AI** shortcut on Home. To learn more, see [Plane AI](/ai/pi-chat) on the web.

## Chat

- Start a new conversation and ask Plane AI a question in plain language.
- Keep separate **threads** for different topics. Rename a thread, mark it as a favorite, or delete it.
- Your recent and favorited threads are easy to return to.

## Set the context

Point Plane AI at the right data by setting a **context**. You can scope a conversation to your **workspace** or to a specific **project** so answers stay relevant to what you are working on.

## See how it thinks

When Plane AI works through a question, you can expand its **thoughts** to see the reasoning behind an answer. After a response, give **feedback** with a thumbs up or thumbs down to help it improve.

## Take actions with Build mode

When **Build mode** is on, Plane AI can do more than answer. It can create work items, cycles, and other objects for you. On mobile, it proposes the actions first and asks you to **confirm** before anything runs.

::: info
Build mode is activated from the web app. Once it is on for your workspace, the mobile app lets you confirm and run the proposed actions.
:::

## Voice and files

Where supported, you can speak your question and have it transcribed before sending, and attach a file to a message for Plane AI to work with.

::: info
Plane AI is a Pro feature and is available on recent app versions. Availability can also depend on your workspace's AI settings and, for self-hosted instances, your server version.
:::
