# Pages, Wiki & Collections

Pages are Plane's documents for notes, specs, and knowledge. On mobile you can write project pages, browse your workspace Wiki, and organize pages into Collections.

## Project pages

Open a project and tap **Pages** to see its pages. You can view public, private, and archived pages, mark important ones as favorites, and search by title.

### Create a page

Tap **Create** and choose **Page**, or use the create action inside a project's Pages.

1. Select the project from the project name at the top.
2. Add an icon and a title.
3. Use the toolbar at the bottom to add and format content blocks.
4. Tap **Create** to add it to the project.

You can create a page as **public** to the project or as a **private** page only you can see.

### Work with a page

- **Favorite** a page with the star icon for quick access.
- **Share** a page with a link.
- **Archive** a page to take it out of the active list, and **Restore** it later.
- **Delete** a page. If it has sub-pages, they are removed too, and the action cannot be undone.
- **Nested pages.** Pages can hold sub-pages, and you can move between them using the breadcrumb at the top.

### Access levels

Your access to a page depends on how it was shared. You may have **view-only access** to read a page, **comment access** to read and comment, or full access to edit. Locked pages are read-only until they are unlocked.

### Comments

Open a page's comments to discuss it inline. You can reply in threads and resolve or reopen a comment once it is handled.

::: info
Page comments, shared access, and nested pages depend on your plan and server version. Some advanced editor blocks, such as AI blocks, require a Business plan.
:::

## Wiki <Badge type="info" text="Pro" />

The **Wiki** is your workspace-level knowledge base, separate from project pages. Open it from the Wiki shortcut on Home. You can browse shared and archived wiki pages, search across them, and read nested pages. To learn more, see [Wiki](/core-concepts/pages/wiki) on the web.

::: info
Wiki is available from self-hosted `v1.8.1`.
:::

## Collections <Badge type="info" text="Pro" />

**Collections** group related wiki pages so your knowledge base stays organized.

- **Create a collection** with a name, an icon, and an access level.
- Open a collection to see the pages inside it.
- Add pages to a collection to keep a topic together.

To learn more, see [Collections](/pages/collections) on the web.
