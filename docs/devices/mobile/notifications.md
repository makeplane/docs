# Notifications

The **Inbox** keeps you up to date on the work that matters to you. Tap **Inbox** in the navigation bar to open it. A badge on the icon shows how many notifications are unread. To learn the concept, see [Notifications](/communication-and-collaboration/notifications) on the web.

## Filter your Inbox

Narrow the list to find what you need:

- **All** shows every notification.
- **Unread** shows only what you have not read yet.
- **Mentions** shows only notifications where you were mentioned.

You can also filter by how a notification reached you:

- **Assigned to me**
- **Created by me**
- **Subscribed by me**

## Act on notifications

- Tap a notification to open the item it refers to and automatically mark the notification as read.
- Tap **Mark all read** to mark all unread notifications as read.

You are notified about activity on work items such as when they are created, updated, or deleted.

## Push notifications

::: info
Push notifications are currently available only for Plane Cloud users.
:::

Turn on push notifications to be alerted on your device about relevant updates. Allow notifications when the app prompts you, or enable them later in your device settings. For a quick, swipeable digest of unread activity, see [Catch-up](/devices/mobile/home#catch-up).
